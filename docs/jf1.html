🔢 Understanding About Number ?

Numbers are special symbols that we can use to count, measure,and understand how much
of something we have, like counting toys or knowing how many cookies are left.

⭐ What is whole number ?

Whole numbers represent complete and full objects. They are numbers without any decimal point.

Examples: 1 2 3 5 7

🍎 Counting Fruits

# If you have 4 apples, each apple is complete.
# You cannot say 4 apples if one apple is half eaten.

🍎 = 1
Amount = 🍎 + 🍎 + 🍎 + 🍎 Print("There's",Amount,"Apples")

Output :There's 4 Apples

👉 4 is a whole number because it shows complete apples.

🎒 School Items

# You bring 6 books to school.
# Books are counted as complete objects.

📕 = 1
Amount = 📕 + 📕 + 📕 + 📕 + 📕 + 📕 Print("There's",Amount,"Books")

Output : There's 6 Books

👉 6 is a whole number because it shows total complete books

⭐ What is Decimal Number?

Decimal numbers show parts of a whole. They always contain a decimal point (.).

Examples: 1.5 2.3 4.7 6.1

🍕 Sharing Pizza

# You already eat one and half pizza
# Half pizza can be written as 0.5 pizza.

🍕 = 0.5
Amount = 🍕 + 🍕 + 🍕
Print("You totally eat",Amount,"Pizza")

Output : You totally eat 1.5 pizza

👉 1.5 is decimal, because it shows full and half pizza.

🍐 Avocado Juice

# You need 250 gram avocado to make a juice.
# 1 avocado have 100 gram in weight

🍐 = 1, 🥑 = 0.5
Amount = 🍐 + 🍐 + 🥑
Print("You need",Amount,"Avocado")

Output : You need 2.5 Avocado

👉 2.5 is decimal. it shows 2 full + half avocado
